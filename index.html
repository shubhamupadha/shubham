Welcome To Divine Providence High School

Divine Providence High School, your child’s second home has been Shaping minds and Touching lives. Rahul Education started in 1992 with just one school in Bhayandar. Today, 30+ years down the line, it has grown to include a chain of over 55+ institutions that have impacted over 55000+ young lives. You can find Rahul Education branches in most of the major cities in India, namely Thane (Bhayander), Mira Road, Nallasopara, Palghar Pune, Varanasi.

OUR HISTORY

Divine Providence High School, Nallasopara East was established in the year 2005 with a noble objective of Quality education. The school is recognized by the Government of Maharashtra and is affiliated to the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education. The School is a part of Rahul Education Group that boasts of excellent infrastructure, qualified & committed teachers. We provide our students with a safe learning environment that helps them grow as unique personalities. We know that each child is different and hence a one size fits all teaching method would never do. We employ several innovative teaching techniques that impart knowledge and instill strong values into the child to become responsible future citizens of the world. Along with traditional lessons, we encourage our students to open their minds to a myriad of extracurricular activities, from Music and Dance to swimming and games. We believe in the child finding their happy place in school; it doesn’t matter if it’s in the science lab or the Football field. Today, the school has completed 16 years and has been one of the best State board schools in the vicinity to provide finest education to its students.

OUR FACULTY

A school is only as good as its teachers, and as one of the finest State Board Schools in your vicinity, we know we have to hire the best. Every teacher in our team must undergo a rigorous hiring process before attending the in-house teachers training program. Apart from observing lessons, the teachers have to give assessments to ensure their subject matter knowledge is up to our standards.

We also train our teachers to adopt our teaching methodologies and ensure that they regularly participate in curriculum workshops and subject-specific seminars to stay updated at all times.
